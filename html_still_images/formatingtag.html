Tiruchirappalli (Trichy) is an ancient city in Tamil Nadu, India, located in the state's geographic center on the banks of the Cauvery River, known for the historic Rock Fort. It is a significant cultural and industrial hub with a rich history, having been ruled by the Cholas, Pandyas, and the British. Key attractions include the Rock Fort, the Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple, and the Kallanai Dam, making it a popular destination for religious and historical tourism.
